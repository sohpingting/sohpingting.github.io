    Answer Rating Insights 2017

    The feature of the answer rating allows attendees to rate the quality of the answer given to their questions. I did the answer rating report design and a pop-over guide to teach speakers when and how to use the rating feature.

    Another designer and I took around two months to research, design, develop and ship the first version of the Answer Rating feature. This feature is a supplement to the existing flow and intends to promote the use of the existing "Answered" function, but the uptake is very low. After some investigation on user usage and behaviour, I made some improvements, and the usage increase by 37% (1 month after release).

    Preventing user error 2017

    When the user wants to add a new speaker profile to the session of their event, they were confused by the two call-to-action buttons, 'Add new speaker' and 'Save'. If they click "Save' button before clicking on 'Add new speaker', it will close the modal, save the selected speaker but not the new speaker profile. The users were frustrated because they need to enter the speaker profile again.

    The improvement has prevented the user from making the mistake of clicking on the wrong call-to-action button.

    Mood Slider 2016

    While working in the Innovation Centre, we focus on learning and creating innovation that can disrupt the traditional market. Moodyslide is one of the projects on helping phone-obsessed millennials to record and discover the trigger for their mood.

    We have three months to prove the concept by having at least 100 users. I've helped to research, design, build and test Moodyslide with the team of Product Owner, Creative Director, Web and Mobile Phone Developers. We have proven the concept to work but couldn't acquire 100 users within 3 months. After 6 months we have gotten 238 users but have to discontinue Moodyslide because we can't monetise it.

    SmoovPay's Payme 2014

    Payme is a mobile payment app that allows merchants to collect payment on their mobile device. The project aims to eliminate the complexity of code integration and the hassle of filling up long payment collection form.

    Outcome achieved: Merchants feedback that it's convenient to collect payment while on the move. Their average transaction also increased by 10%.
