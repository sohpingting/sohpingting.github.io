    Answer Rating Insights 2017

    TThe feature of answer rating allows attendees to rate the quality of the answer given to their questions. I did the answer rating report design and a pop-over guide to teach speakers when and how to use the rating feature.

    This project took around two months to research, develop and ship. After it's release, we observed the usage behaviour and improved area that is lacking. Since the improvement of feature hint, answer rating was used 37% more.

    Mood Slider 2016

    In the Innovation Centre, we focus on testing and learning new ideas and business model in the exploration of innovation. Moodyslide is one of the projects which I've helped to research, design, build, test and learn. We have three months to prove the idea by having at least 100 users.

    After rounds of reframing the problem and ideation, it became the mood tracking app today with 238 users. Although we have proven the idea, the app has to discontinue due to a business decision.

    SmoovPay's Payme 2014

    Payme is a mobile payment app that allows merchants to collect payment on their mobile device. The project aims to eliminate the complexity of code integration and the hassle of filling up long payment collection form.

    Outcome achieved: Merchants feedback that it's convenient to collect payment while on the move. Their average transaction also increased by 10%.
