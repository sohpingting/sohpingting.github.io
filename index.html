What We Do

We are Asia's first post accelerator.

We prepare young companies with disruptive technologies, products and services to sell and partner with large corporates. We help start-ups navigate the B2B sales cycle.

Programme

Our 3 month programme is focused on getting you to the big win - a contract from CoLife. We help you sharpen your proposition and connect you the right people at CoLife.

Customer

Connect with your potential customers. Sharpen your insight.

B2B Sales

Understand how to build a sales organization.

Finance and Legal

Learn how to structure contracts to work with large corporations.




Mentorship

LumenLab and our external experts offer immense industry and subject matter expertise, and will guide startups on technology, products, and business.

Customer and Partner Access

Startups gain access to LumenLab and their partner facing events and to select customers or partners for proofs-of-concept or demos.

Pilot

One startup out of the cohort of 8 is guaranteed a revenue pilot of US$100,000. That's not all, more than one startup could win!

Investor Connections

Interactions with the angel investor and venture capital community give startups the right direction to fuel your growth story.

Who Can Apply?

If you are a start-up that shares our vision to enhance the accessibility of insurance to customers (including the underserved and unserved communities).

If you would like to improve user experience and create new innovative business models – you are what we’re looking for.
